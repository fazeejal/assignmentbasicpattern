Instructions

  1. Prepare the ingredients by gathering fresh vegetables, herbs, and proteins.
  2. Chop the vegetables and herbs into bite-sized pieces for your dish.
  3. Marinate the proteins with your favorite seasonings and let them sit for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Heat a pan on medium heat and sauté the vegetables until they are tender.
  5. Cook the marinated proteins until they are fully cooked, and then combine them with the sautéed vegetables.
